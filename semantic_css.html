Home

The theme of this website is nature-inspired, characterized by earthy green tones and a serene waterfall-themed background image in the header.

The design emphasizes simplicity and accessibility, with a clean and organized layout that uses semantic HTML elements like "Home," "Article," "Section," and "Footer" to structure the content clearly. The navigation bar is intuitive and user-friendly, reinforcing the website's focus on clarity and usability.

The overall aesthetic evokes a sense of calmness and connection to nature.
